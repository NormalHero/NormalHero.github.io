ETrade Account Activation

ETrade’s Stock Plan account activation process

ETrade’s complete redesign of their account activation system that was over a decade old. Old C Tuxedo webservices were migrated to our new in-house Java webservices framework. New batch jobs were created to take care of picking up new customer account activations requests periodically. The redesign created a more fluid and intuitive process for the customers while at the same time updating our codebase to a newer tech stack.

EatHarmony

Helping you choose what to eat next

A collaboration project with 3 other developers to create an Android app. EatHarmony presents users with an image of a type of food and the user can either “like” the food by swiping right or “skipping” the food by swiping left. If a user likes the food presented to them and they swipe right, they are given links to Yelp and various restaurants serving that type of food. Histories of their food choices and charts to easily see their choices are also presented to the users.

Network Scanner

Automated network protection

This purpose of this console application is to scan a network and retrieve information about the devices that are currently connected to the network. This application allowed the IT managers at UCSB to monitor connected user devices, keep a history of devices in a database, and alert of any unauthorized devices connecting to the network. UI was not a concern as this console application was automated to run periodically from the command line.

Audio Visualizer

Audio visualizations using Processing 3

As someone who loves music, I experimented with combining music with visualizations. The center ellipse expands and contracts in regards to high treble frequencies while the outer ellipse responds to lower bass frequencies. The Fast Fourier Transformation (FFT) is also used to create the waveform that spans the width of the visualizer. Using Processing 3 and the Minim library, this audio visualizer was made as a concept of how we can use technology, math, and science to visualize music.

Integer Factorization

Research on factorization methods used in modern day cryptographic systems

This paper describes in detail modern factorization methods and their relation to popular cryptographic systems. The consequences of finding faster and cheaper factorization methods has devastating real world implications as the security of our personal emails, bank accounts, etc. depend on the inherent difficulty of factoring large semi-prime numbers. This paper explores the number theory and mathematical concepts used in common factorization methods and how prime factorization plays a key role in current cryptographic systems.
